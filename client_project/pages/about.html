About The Creator- Garrett Dato

Garrett is currently a high school student graduating in the class of 2022. Garrett started Royalty Rugz as a side hobby making a couple rugz for friends and eventually selling them to fellow classmates. He then decided to take his business to the next level by making a website and social medias to get his brand out there!

"Making rugz isn't something that I ever knew I'd want to do. A few months ago I saw a video online of somebody creating a rug and I thought; 'Hey, I could do that too!' After researching and experimenting I learned the skill and created Royalty Rugz, where I now tuft one of one pieces for anyone to enjoy."

-Garrett Dato
