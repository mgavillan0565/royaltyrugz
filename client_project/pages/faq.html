Frequently Asked Questions

 

 

How long does it take to make one? 

Each rug takes around 3-5 business days. The time varies based on the rug size.     

The larger the rug, the longer it may take.


Is free shipping an option?

Rugz $200 or more get free shipping.


How are the rugz made?

Yarn is tufted through a canvas and glued down to secure the hold that makes a useable, quality rug.


How much do the rugz cost?

Small rugz range from $20-$40

Medium rugz range from $60-$80

Large rugz are $100+
